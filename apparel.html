2022-2023 Winter Apparel
Burton AK Collection Jacket

This jacket specializes in keeping you dry rather than keeping you warm. Jackets like these usually have waterproof ratings labeled 5K, 10K, 20K, and more. The higher the number, the more waterproof it is. This jacket uses GORE-TEX which is approximately a 28K waterproof rating.

Burton Covert Pant

This high-performance pant is made with both waterproofing and insulation in mind. Insulation is usually measured in gram weight. The heavier the gram weight, the warmer it is. This pant is made with 10K waterproofing and 40g insulation. It's great for cold winter days to keep you warm.

MORE

Burton Coaches Jacket

This coaches jacket is a nod to the retro varsity style jackets in sports. It looks plain, but it contains waterproofing tech and fleece lined pockets to help keep you dry and warm. Like the Melter pant, it focuses on warmer weather days for maximum comfort and breathability.

MORE
Burton Melter Pant

The melter pant is lightweight and loose-fitting for maximum comfort. These pants focus on warmer weather days like in the spring so there is no insulation. It has a waterproof rating of approximately 10K to keep costs low and maintain maximum breathability. Currently, jogger style pants are trending, so it has elastic gaiters.
